Kidney Function Tests (RFT/KFT)

This profile evaluates the kidneys' ability to filter waste from the blood. Below are the core parameters:

1. Serum Creatinine

A waste product from muscle metabolism, filtered by the kidneys.

Normal Range: 0.6 – 1.2 mg/dL

2. Blood Urea

Reflects protein metabolism; high levels often indicate renal stress.

Normal Range: 15 – 45 mg/dL

3. Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN)

Measures the amount of nitrogen in the blood that comes from urea.

Normal Range: 7 – 20 mg/dL

4. eGFR (Estimated Glomerular Filtration Rate)

The most accurate test to determine the stage of kidney disease based on creatinine, age, and gender.

Normal Range: > 90 mL/min/1.73m²

*Note: Always consult your physician for clinical interpretation of these results.
